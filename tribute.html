About Steve

Steven Paul Jobs, born steveat San Francisco, California, was an American business magnate, industrial designer, media proprietor, and investor. He was the co-founder, chairman, and CEO of Apple; the chairman and majority shareholder of Pixar; a member to The Walt Disney Company’s board of directors; and the founder, chairman, and CEO of NeXT. he is widely recognized a s a pioneer of the personal computer revolution of the 1970s and 1980s, along with his early business partner and fellow Apple co-founder Steve Wozniak.

Steve's Timeline

  • 1955 - Born in San Francisco, California
  • 1972 - Enrolled at Reed College in Portland, Oregon
  • 1974 - Worked at Atari, Inc in Los Gatos, California
  • 1976- Founded Apple Computer Company with Steve Wozniak
  • 1977 - With Steve Wozniak introduced the Apple II
  • 1985 - Resign from Apple and founded NeXT
  • 1986 - Funded the spinout of The Graphics Group (leter named Pixar) form Lucasfilm’s computer graphics division.
  • 1995 - Produced Toy Story, partnership between Pixar and Disney
  • 1996 - Return to Apple, after Apple buy NeXT, and become CEO.
  • 2003 - Diagnosed with cancer
  • August 24, 2001 - Announced resignation as Apple CEO
  • October 5, 2011 - Died at the age of 56
